Bio Amy Bandman

Amy Bandman joined Harper College in 2014 as the college’s first Campus Sustainability Manager. Through forming strong intercampus connections and utilizing innovative thinking, Amy continues to lead the campus toward its goal of carbon neutrality by 2053. Amy’s focus is on campus engagement and driving participation in environmental action and has led the campus through the development of sustainability programming for students, staff and 23 surrounding communities. Amy is a Network Associate for the Illinois Green Economy Network (IGEN) and a member of the Chicagoland Network of Sustainability in Higher Education (CNSHE). Prior to joining the field of sustainability in higher education, Amy worked as Program Director for River Action, an environmental non-profit, where she developed community education events to engage the public in celebrating the vitality of the Mississippi River. Amy received her Bachelor's degree in Geography from Augustana College and is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Geography and Environmental Studies from Northeastern Illinois University.
